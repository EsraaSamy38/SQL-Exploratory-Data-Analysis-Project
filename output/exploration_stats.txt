Key Findings from Layoffs Analysis:
- Tech sector accounted for 45% of total layoffs (2022-2023)
- Peak layoffs occurred in Q1 2023 (12,000 employees)
- 22 companies had 100% staff reductions
- Monthly rolling total reached 85,000 by 2023-12
- Top 5 companies by layoffs: Amazon, Google, Meta, Microsoft, Salesforce
